Simple ASCII Art Generator (Using Patch Averaging)

this 10 and 70 levels of ascii art are created only using cv.resize function,first we convert the image into greyscale, we then take intended(resize size, for here the column value is 80) size,and resize that after that we take pixel colour value and based on its colour value(0-255) assign it to a charecter.

this 10 and 70 levels of ascii art are created using cv.resize function and patch extraction method ,first we convert the image into greyscale, we then resize to a patch extractable size, so we can easily get 80 column value, and also no point patch, and the aspect ratio are as close as possible, then we take each patch and find its averave colour value and based on its colour value(0-255) assign it to a charecter(for both 10 and 70 levels).

Challenges Faced

The development of ASCII art required overcoming multiple challenges:

  • Image Preprocessing: Ensuring that resizing does not distort the aspect ratio significantly.
  • Character Mapping: Finding the best character set to represent different intensity levels.
  • Computational Complexity: Handling large image and video files efficiently.
  • Storage: Managing the large dataset of frames and intermediate outputs.
